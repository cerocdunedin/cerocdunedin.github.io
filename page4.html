Whether you want a wedding dance that is extra special or private lessons to improve your dancing quickly, read on then get in touch!

WEDDING DANCES

Tom and Sarah offer private lessons for couples wanting to do something extra special for their First Dance.

We work alongside couples to create a unique choreographed sequence to a song of their choice, tailored to suit their personalities, abilities, wedding themes and preferences.

Over the last few years we have successfully worked with many couples in the lead up to their big day and have had great feedback from them. We would love the opportunity to work with you to enhance this aspect of your wedding â€“ this is one of our favourite ways of sharing our love of dance!

The price is $60 per hour session, and most couples have around 4 sessions to learn and polish a dance. We also provide notes and videos of the routine for you, are receptive to messages in between sessions and provide your photographer with a "cheat-sheet" of where we recommend they position themselves to get the best photos possible of your First Dance.

We choreograph for dancers of any level. If you've never done Ceroc before, we encourage you to come along to some beginner classes first (especially making use of our new student 6 for 3 deal!) to learn the basics. This means we'll be able to incorporate more advanced moves into your routine and you're likely to feel more confident learning the sequence rather than the foundations skills and routine at the same time. Plus, learning to "freestyle" will allow you to continue dancing into the night. However, we've also taught many couples the basics alongside a routine with great results!

Please see the testimonials section below for how couples find working with us.

PRIVATE LESSONS

Want specific feedback about how to improve your dancing?

Private lessons are a great way to get individualised advice on how to make your dancing look and feel even better.

Generally the price is $30 for half an hour, or $60 for an hour.
