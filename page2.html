Ceroc Dunedin monthly dance parties

Saturday 18th August - St John's Church Hall
Saturday 22nd September -St John's Church Hall
Saturday 27th October - St John's Church Hall
Saturday 17th November - St John's Church Hall
Friday 14th December - South Dunedin Community Hall
For those who haven’t attended a dance party before:  they are a fantastic evening of socialising and dancing. The evening kicks off with a Beginner class around 7.30pm. Once the class is finished it is time for social dancing, much like Wednesday nights between the classes, but for the remainder of the evening (until midnight, if you wish!). A scrumptious supper plus tea and coffee is served around 10pm to replenish your energy levels. 
Most of our dance parties are at St Johns Church Hall in Roslyn. It has a lovely dance floor and facilities. For those who don’t know where it is please see below.

We look forward to seeing you! Ensure you save us a dance!
