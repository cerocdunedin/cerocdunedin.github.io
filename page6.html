What is Ceroc?

Ceroc Dance is a world-wide organization and here in New Zealand we have many branches across the country. 

Ceroc is a funky fusion of many different dance styles and can be danced to almost any music including current chart-toppers, Latin, blues & swing. 

There is no tricky footwork so it's easy to learn and looks good quickly.

It's fun, social and great exercise! 

Start ANYTIME.

No partner, booking or experience necessary. Walk in, DANCE out!


Ceroc Dunedin

Ceroc Dance has been operating in Dunedin for over 14 years and has been a well loved dance style for many people over the years. We love to provide opportunities to learn to dance and ensure a great social night out.

We pride ourselves on being an inclusive and welcoming community for all - many people comment on the great level of respect, care and family feel.

We have a range of dancers of all ages and backgrounds.  

We recommend being 16 years or older when you join. We have a great group of younger dancers who come to Ceroc.
